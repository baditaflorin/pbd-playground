# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, focused, and welcoming.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, trolling, personal attacks, and publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
